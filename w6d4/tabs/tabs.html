A St. Stephen's Day pie is made using turkey and ham. It's so delicious that I'm often like, "yum".

The Cumberland pie is a variant of Shepherd's Pie with a layer of breadcrumb on top. It's like, so, like, good... that sometimes, I'm like, BREADCRUMBS!? WOW! I like bread too.

Despite it's name, fish pie is not the most disgusting, revolting food dish mankind has ever created. We all know what that is: Eel Pie. I'm mean, eew. The fish pie is usually made with white, often smoked, fish (for example cod, haddock or halibut) in a white sauce or cheddar cheese sauce made using the milk the fish was poached in.
